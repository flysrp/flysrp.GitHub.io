Democracy vs Republic

發佈於 2020-02-19

The idea of “rule by majority” is terrible. What if the election result yields 51% over 49% and the 51% wants the rest 49% to be hanged. Should that request be fulfilled under majority?
Therefore the modern meaning of democracy is deviant from its original sense “rule by majority”. But rather, modern democracy is more like to be defined as the classical idea of Republic, which is “rule by everyone”.
