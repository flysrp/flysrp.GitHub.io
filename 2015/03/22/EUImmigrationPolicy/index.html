欧盟难民政策

2015-03-22 文字

大量来自非洲和中亚的难民想尽各种方法偷渡入欧洲。主流媒体和政府的态度都以部分接纳为主。TE更指出老龄化的欧洲需要一些新鲜劳动力的注入。下图是难民偷渡方式的分析：


地中海提供了无尽的希望，但也会无情的吞噬生命。最近一张溺亡后被冲到沙滩上幼童照片更是激起全世界的怜悯。无论国家政策如何，年轻的生命绝不该如此失去。其他类似的大批难民在偷渡过程中死亡的新闻不胜枚举。

下图是全世界几个主要移民接收国对移民的处理方式：


不得不说瑞士德国这些国家还是秉持着日耳曼血统中最珍贵的存在。但开明的移民政策（尤其是申根国家内人员流通性大得特点）被越来越多的新移民当做漏洞一下利用。

抛开怜悯心的话，从非洲和中亚逃来的难民简直一无是处。以下是节选自一篇愤慨的网友评论：

Because the political class and magazines like this prefer to hide behind political correctness and ignore what’s going on. They disregard the street harassment women in Birmingham face walking down the street in a skirt. They ignore the massive increase of rapes this past decade among Swedish women in Stockholm. They bluster when shown that 70% of France’s prison population is Muslim. That half of all British muslim men and 75% of all muslim women are unemployed. That 80% of Sweden’s welfare budget goes to foreigners or foreign born. That as many as a quarter of all Muslims in the west support terrorism and suicide attacks in the name of Islam. They love to pretend that these immigrants are nothing more than job seeking labor-bots without their own cultures and compulsions, who won’t change the fabric of society, and will simply add to the economy.

Sorry, the regular people around the West see exactly what is going on. They saw the Italian aid worker was gang-raped at a migrant center two weeks ago. They saw that a bunch of Muslims migrants attacking police and trying to kill a man in Germany after he tore pages out of the Quran last week. They know 800,000 migrants to Germany alone in one year is not sustainable or healthy. They aren’t stupid. They aren’t blind. And if you continue to lie to them, you will lose all legitimacy, and things will get way worse before they get better.
People have a right to know the truth about what is going on. People have a right to preserve their cultural heritage and community cohesion. And people have a right to not spend their money on migrants who far too often mooch off of the dole.

这也侧面说明了为什么这几年在英国，瑞士，德国等发达欧洲国家采取反对移民甚至退出欧盟立场（特指英国，卡梅伦已经宣布2017年前举行全面公投决定是否退出欧盟）的政党支持率越来越高。
